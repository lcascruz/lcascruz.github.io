INFO

I am a PhD candidate at the University of Cambridge, where I study how language is represented in the brain, particularly in bilingual individuals. Relatedly, my interests also focus on how language processing interacts with broader cognitive systems, including memory and attention. I completed master's degrees in Brazil and Italy with top honours, and have conducted research across institutions in Spain, the Netherlands, and the UK, where I have explored topics in semantic processing, prediction in bilingual comprehension, and cognitive effects of bilingualism.
